About me

Presently, I hold the position of a full-time Tech Lead and Senior Software Development Engineer II, where I lead teams and contribute to the technological evolution of projects.

This journey has been particularly rewarding, having had the opportunity to work closely with Amazon sellers, understanding their unique challenges and delivering tailored solutions.

Being inherently passionate about technology, I thrive on staying ahead of the curve. I am well-versed in a spectrum of trending tech stacks, and I dedicate myself to continuous learning, ensuring that I am always abreast of the latest advancements in the tech landscape. This commitment to staying informed empowers me to bring innovative and future-proof solutions to the projects I undertake.
